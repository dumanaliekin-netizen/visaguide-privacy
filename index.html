Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 4, 2026

VisaGuide ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

  • Account Information: Your email address when you create an account.
  • Document Images: Photos and scans of visa-related documents (passports, financial statements, etc.) that you voluntarily upload for AI-assisted verification.
  • Interview Practice Data: Text answers you type during visa interview practice sessions, along with application context details you have previously entered (such as purpose of visit, intended stay duration, and employer information), used to generate AI coaching feedback.
  • Application Data: Visa application details you enter, including visa type, target country, intended stay duration, and appointment dates, stored to power your checklists and reminders.
  • AI Consent Record: When you give explicit consent for AI document processing, we store a record of your consent including the consent date and version of the consent text. You can review or revoke this at any time from Profile > Data & Privacy.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the app, including features accessed and actions taken, collected automatically to improve the service.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and app version for technical support and compatibility purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your account.
  • Provide AI-assisted document verification services.
  • Provide AI-powered visa interview practice coaching and feedback.
  • Display your visa application checklists and track progress.
  • Schedule appointment reminders and document deadline alerts.
  • Improve and personalise the application experience.
  • Respond to your requests and provide customer support.
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations.

3. AI-Powered Features

VisaGuide uses Anthropic's Claude AI for two features:

Document Verification: Before your document image is processed by AI, we ask for your explicit consent as required by KVKK and applicable data protection law. Your consent is recorded with a timestamp in your account. When you request AI verification, the image is transmitted to Anthropic's Claude API via a secure Firebase Cloud Function. Your document image is permanently deleted from our servers immediately after AI analysis is complete — only the verification result (pass/fail, confidence score, issues list) is retained. You can revoke this consent at any time from Profile > Data & Privacy.
Interview Practice Coaching: When you submit a practice answer in the Interview Prep feature, your typed answer text and relevant application context (visa type, purpose of visit, intended stay, employer name, and whether you have family in the destination country) are transmitted to Anthropic's Claude API via a secure Firebase Cloud Function. The AI returns a coaching result — including a score, identified risk phrases, improvement suggestions, and a model answer — which is saved to your account. Each user is limited to 20 AI coaching checks per day.

In both cases, Anthropic processes the submitted data solely to return a result and does not store your data for its own training purposes beyond what its own privacy policy specifies. Please review Anthropic's Privacy Policy at https://www.anthropic.com/privacy for details.

4. Local Notifications

If you enable reminders, VisaGuide uses local notifications on your device to alert you about:

  • Upcoming passport expiry dates that you enter in the app (at 6 months, 3 months, and 1 month before expiry).
  • Missing documents for your active visa applications.
  • Upcoming visa appointment dates.

All notification scheduling is performed locally on your device. You can disable reminders at any time in Profile > Notifications, or through your device's Settings > Notifications.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase (Google LLC) — Authentication, database storage (Firestore), and file storage (Cloud Storage).
    https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Anthropic PBC — AI document analysis and interview coaching via the Claude API.
    https://www.anthropic.com/privacy

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

6. Data Retention

Document images uploaded for AI verification are deleted from our servers immediately after processing is complete — they are never stored beyond the moment needed for analysis.

All other personal data is retained for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide our services. You may delete your account and all associated data at any time through the Profile > Danger Zone section of the app. Upon account deletion, your data is permanently removed from our systems within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS), Firebase Security Rules that restrict data access to the account owner only, and secure cloud infrastructure. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

8. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: Delete all your data or your entire account directly within the app, or by contacting us.
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format.
  • Objection: Object to certain types of processing.
  • Revoke AI Consent: Withdraw your consent for AI document processing at any time from Profile > Data & Privacy. Revoking consent disables the AI verification feature until you re-consent.

To exercise these rights, use the in-app account management features or contact us at the address below.

9. Children's Privacy

VisaGuide is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or 16 in the European Economic Area). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately so we can delete it.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence, including the United States, where our service providers operate. We ensure that such transfers comply with applicable data protection laws through appropriate safeguards.

11. KVKK — Turkish Data Protection

For users in Turkey, VisaGuide complies with the Kişisel Verilerin Korunması Kanunu (KVKK — Law No. 6698 on the Protection of Personal Data).

Legal basis for processing your personal data under KVKK:

  • Account data (email, name): Necessary for performance of the service contract.
  • Document images for AI verification: Based on your explicit consent, obtained through the in-app consent screen before the feature is first used.
  • Application data and usage data: Legitimate interest in providing and improving the service.

Data controller: VisaGuide (privacy@visaguide.app)

You may exercise your rights under KVKK Article 11 — including the right to learn whether your data is processed, request correction or deletion, and object to processing — by contacting us at privacy@visaguide.app.

Your continued use of the app after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
